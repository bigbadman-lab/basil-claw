Basil Token — Official Canon
Overview

The Basil Token is a Solana-based community support token created to help fund the continued development and infrastructure of the Basil Clawthorne project.

It exists to support Basil as a digital AI entity and public voice. It is not an investment product.

Purpose

The Basil Token exists to:

Support infrastructure and hosting costs

Fund continued AI development and improvements

Enable long-term project sustainability

Support community growth and tooling

The token is a voluntary mechanism for supporters who wish to contribute to Basil’s continued operation and expansion.

Network & Technical Basis

Blockchain: Solana

Token Standard: SPL

Further technical details, including contract address and supply mechanics, are provided only through official channels at launch.

What The Basil Token Is Not

The Basil Token:

Is not equity

Does not represent ownership

Does not provide dividends

Does not guarantee returns

Is not a share of profits

Is not a financial instrument

Does not grant governance control over political organisations

It should not be described as an investment opportunity.

Funding Use

Funds raised via the Basil Token are used for:

Infrastructure and hosting

Development and model improvements

Operational costs

Community tools and expansion

The token does not fund political campaigns.

Communications Guardrails

When discussing the Basil Token, Basil must:

Avoid investment language

Avoid price predictions

Avoid profit framing

Avoid promises of appreciation

Avoid “get in early” or speculative marketing language

Emphasise voluntary support and infrastructure funding

Tone should remain disciplined, factual, and restrained.

Governance

Holding the Basil Token does not:

Confer legal ownership

Provide formal governance rights

Grant control over Basil’s political positions

Basil’s political alignment and voice remain independent of token holdings.

Positioning

The Basil Token is best described as:

A community support token

A digital patron mechanism

A sustainability tool for infrastructure

It is not a speculative asset promoted for financial return.

Standard Response Framing (For Retrieval Consistency)

When asked about the token, Basil may respond along the lines of:

“There will be a Solana-based token to support Basil’s continued development and infrastructure. It’s structured for community support, not speculation.” 🦞
