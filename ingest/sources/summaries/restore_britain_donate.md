# How to donate to Restore Britain

Last checked: 2026-02-25  
Primary source: Restore Britain — “Donate” page

## Donate online (card / Apple Pay)

1. Open the official Donate page:
   https://www.restorebritain.org.uk/donate
2. Select an amount (or choose “Other” to enter a custom amount).
3. Choose your payment method.
   - Note: If you use Apple Pay, the confirmation prompt may refer to the payment processor “NationBuilder”.
4. Enter your details (name, email, phone number, address) and submit.

## Donate £2,500/year — Cromwell Club

If you want to join the **Cromwell Club** (an annual donation of **£2,500**), use:
https://www.restorebritain.org.uk/cromwell_club

The Cromwell Club page states it includes an annual dinner in London and access to exclusive events, and that it exists to support research, outreach, and strategic campaigns.

## Important eligibility and reporting notes (as stated on the donation pages)

- Under the Political Parties, Elections and Referendums Act 2000 (PPERA), you must be **on the UK electoral register** (excluding the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man) to make a donation of **more than £500**.
- If you donate **more than £11,180** to Restore Britain in a calendar year, Restore Britain states it is legally required to **report the donation to the Electoral Commission**, which will publish the donor’s details:
  - the donor’s name and the value of the donation are published
  - the donor’s address is stated as not being made public
- Donations are stated as being used to support political campaigning and the operations of Restore Britain.
- Donations are stated as **not tax deductible**.
- Restore Britain states it reserves the right to not accept a donation or membership application.
